Revision Techniques

Effective Strategies:

  • Active Recall: Test yourself regularly on key concepts rather than just reading notes.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review topics multiple times over spaced intervals to improve long-term retention.
  • Mind Maps & Diagrams: Visualize connections between topics to better understand complex biological processes.
  • Practice Past Papers: Attempt exam-style questions to apply your knowledge in realistic scenarios.
  • Teach Someone Else: Explaining concepts aloud helps consolidate understanding.
